Features

Developed SGIC-Net, a deep learning-based underwater multimedia compression system
using a Convolutional Autoencoder (CAE) and Savitzky-Golay filtering in MATLAB.
The model efficiently compressed and reconstructed underwater images and audio
with high quality, outperforming traditional methods
like JPEG, PCA, MP3, and AAC on key metrics such as PSNR, SSIM, and SNR.
